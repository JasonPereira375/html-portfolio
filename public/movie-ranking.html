My 3 Favorite Movies

Ford v Ferrari

If you like cars you will like this movie.
This is one of the most famous true stories of racing history retold with everything that makes a good movie.

Luca

My favorite Pixar movie.
It is colorful, funny, and always gives you a renewed wonder and excitement for the world and life by the end.

Spider-man: Into the Spider-Verse

Good animation, Good music, Good story
